# Welcome to My Digital Garden

This is more than just a website—it's a **digital garden** where ideas grow, connect, and evolve. Unlike traditional blogs with chronological posts, this space is organized around relationships and connections.

## What You'll Find Here

### 📝 Thoughts & Reflections
- Technical insights and tutorials
- Personal reflections on technology and life  
- Ideas about software development, design, and creativity
- Explorations of interesting problems and solutions

### 🛠️ Projects & Experiments
- Web applications and mobile apps
- Open source contributions
- Creative coding experiments
- Professional work (where shareable)

### 🧠 The Knowledge Graph
The most unique feature of this site is the **interactive graph** that shows how everything connects. Click on any thought, project, or skill to see its relationships to other content.

## How to Navigate

1. **Browse by Section**: Use the navigation to explore thoughts, projects, or my resume
2. **Follow Connections**: Look for connection indicators and related content
3. **Explore the Graph**: Visit the graph view to see the big picture
4. **Search & Filter**: Find content by topics, technologies, or themes

## The Philosophy

I believe that knowledge isn't just a collection of isolated facts—it's a network of interconnected ideas. This site reflects that philosophy by making connections explicit and explorable.

Every piece of content here connects to others through:
- **Shared technologies** used in different projects
- **Related concepts** explored in multiple thoughts
- **Skills developed** through various experiences
- **Inspirations** that sparked new ideas
